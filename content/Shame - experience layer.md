# Shame — Experience Layer

Shame is not just a concept. It is a shift in awareness and contact.

## Phenomenology
- A turning inward of attention
- Reduction in contact with others
- A sense of exposure or contraction

## In the therapeutic field
Shame often appears when [[Contact]] is disrupted or when [[Boundary]] feels unsafe.

## Body process
- Collapsing posture
- Reduced breath
- Eyes lowering or avoidance

## Related processes
- [[Anxiety]]
- [[Embodiment]]

## Note
This is not a definition. It is a description of lived process.
